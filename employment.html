McKenna Montessori

Teaching Position:

McKenna Charter School is seeking an innovative teacher Certified in Elementary Education to join the staff at our McKenna Montessori. We are interested in an enthusiastic individual that is highly motivated to help students in a multi-age/ level classroom in a Montessori setting. The right person will be a hardworking and ambitious teaching professional. Knowledge of the Montessori philosophy is a plus, but not required. The right applicant may be provided the required training. 

 

The position will involve teaching students ages 6-9 and coordinating with other teachers to offer activities and lessons applicable to appropriate ages and levels. The successful candidate will be an inspired individual with a keen interest in encouraging and inciting students to love learning and excel in the Montessori methods.

Teacher's Assistant:

McKenna Charter School is seeking an energetic person as an assistant to a Certified Teacher on the staff at our McKenna Montessori. We are looking for an enthusiastic individual that is highly motivated to help students in a multi-age/ level classroom in a Montessori setting. The right person will be hardworking and ambitious and willing to help the classroom teacher in all aspects. Knowledge of the Montessori philosophy is a plus, but not required. 

 

The position will involve helping with a classroom of students ages 6-9 and coordinating with other teachers and staff to offer activities and lessons applicable to appropriate ages and levels. The successful candidate will be an inspired individual with a keen interest in encouraging and inciting students to love learning and excel in the Montessori methods.
